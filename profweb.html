Hello! Welcome to my website. Enjoy the vibes I carefully curated for your user navigating pleasure. The color scheme I chose to go with when doing this project was to utilize the cool tones. That being blues and greens. Not only are blue/green my favorite colors, but it is understood that color can invoke powerful emotions. An article that was written by Christi Wharton titled The Psychology of Design: The Color Green states; “Color has been known to have a powerful psychological impact on people’s behavior and decisions. And this knowledge has been harnessed all too well in marketing psychology by designers and marketers alike. Color can often be the sole reason someone purchases a product. In a survey, 93 percent of buyers said they focus on visual appearance, and close to 85 percent claim color is a primary reason when they make a purchase!”

Studies done reveal that these colors evoke the feeling of calmness, security, peace, and rest. On my profile website, I want any user that visits to feel this way while also reading my accomplishments.
